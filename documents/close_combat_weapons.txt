THE CHAINSWORD

Overview:
The Chainsword is the most common close-combat weapon of the Adeptus Astartes. It is a single-handed sword with a motorised chain track lining the blade — hundreds of individual adamantine teeth circulating at high speed. Against unarmoured or lightly armoured targets, the Chainsword is supremely lethal. Against power-armoured opponents, it requires concentrated pressure at weakened joints and seams.

Firepower (Melee):
The Chainsword excels against infantry, daemons, and xenos. Its teeth are serrated titanium-alloy tipped with adamantine. The chain's momentum multiplies the force of each strike. It is less effective than power weapons against heavily armoured opponents.

Length: Standard pattern, approximately 90 cm blade.
One-handed operation: Yes. Typically paired with Bolt Pistol.

Rarity: COMMON. Standard-issue to Assault Marines, Veterans, and many officers. Widely produced and simple to maintain.

Maintenance:
- Inspect chain-track tension after every engagement. A loose chain will shed teeth.
- Clear chain track of debris (bone, armour fragments, flesh) using a pick tool before re-engagement.
- Replace worn chain-links regularly. Teeth that have lost their cutting edge should be replaced in blocks.
- The motor housing must be kept clear of blood and fluid contamination — use GX-4 solvent to flush.
- If the chain binds mid-engagement, the emergency release (red lever, handle guard) disengages the chain drive so the weapon can be used as a standard blade.

SECTION 2 — POWER WEAPONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Power Weapons are standard-bladed or impact weapons (swords, axes, mauls, lances, fists) encased in a disruptive energy field generated by a compact power generator in the hilt or housing. On contact, the energy field disrupts molecular bonds, cutting or crushing through almost any material with significantly less physical force required.

POWER SWORD:
A single or double-edged blade approximately 80–110 cm. The energy field gives the blade an ethereal glow. Extremely effective against armoured targets and vehicles. Favoured by officers and veterans. One-handed operation possible for Astartes.
Rarity: UNCOMMON. Not mass-produced at the scale of bolters. Reserved for squad leaders and officers.

POWER AXE:
A single-bladed power weapon. Heavier, slower, and more destructive per hit than the Power Sword. Severs limbs and penetrates tank-grade armour. Favoured by warriors who prefer raw impact over precision.
Rarity: UNCOMMON.

POWER MAUL:
A one-handed bludgeoning power weapon. Excellent against Chaos-tainted creatures, daemons, and xenos with regenerative biology. The disruptive field scrambles regenerative tissue. Used by Chaplains (as the Crozius Arcanum).
Rarity: UNCOMMON.

POWER FIST:
A gauntlet that encases the hand in an armoured housing surrounded by a disruptive power field. The Power Fist crushes vehicle hulls and smashes through fortifications. Extreme force. Two-handed grip required in practice, though technically one-handed for Terminators. Rate of strike is slower than bladed weapons.
Rarity: UNCOMMON-RARE. Primarily issued to sergeants, veterans, and Terminators.

LIGHTNING CLAWS:
Paired Power Fist gauntlets with extended power-field clades (talons) in place of crushing fists. Lightning Claws are designed for rapid, repeated strikes — trading raw crushing power for speed. Favoured by assault veterans and Terminator assault units.
Rarity: RARE. Complex to manufacture. Two units required for standard deployment (a matched pair).

SECTION 3 — THUNDER HAMMER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The Thunder Hammer is one of the most destructive melee weapons in the Astartes arsenal. It is a massive two-handed (or one-handed for Terminators) hammer that discharges a focused blast of electromagnetic energy upon impact. The discharge is powerful enough to destroy a main battle tank, crush a Daemon's physical form, or simply obliterate anything short of a Titan.

Firepower (Melee):
On impact the Thunder Hammer discharges a concussive energy burst approximately 0.5 metres in radius. It staggers or stuns even targets not directly struck. Against vehicles, it is lethal in most cases. Against super-heavy targets, repeated strikes are required.

Drawback: The weapon is heavy and the energy discharge requires a recharge cycle of approximately 1.5 seconds per strike. It is not a weapon for the impatient. Users who miss completely are dangerously exposed.

Rarity: RARE. Complex internal capacitor and discharge mechanism. Reserved for Terminators, veteran sergeants, and heroes of the Chapter.

SECTION 4 — POWER WEAPON MAINTENANCE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

General Power Weapon Maintenance Protocol:
1. Deactivate the power cell by disengaging the thumb-switch on the grip before all maintenance.
2. Inspect the power field emitter nodes on the blade/surface for damage. Cracked nodes must be replaced — they create localised field voids and can cause the weapon to spit feedback arcs at the wielder.
3. Check the power coupling in the hilt or housing for secure seating. A loose coupling is a fire hazard.
4. Clean the blade with a non-conductive cloth. Do NOT use metallic scrubbers — residual conductivity causes field anomalies.
5. Test the discharge cycle using the diagnostic mode (hold the activation switch for 3 seconds) before combat engagement.
6. Replace the power cell after every 50 activations or as directed by the Armoury.

Chainsword-Specific Maintenance:
1. Disengage power to chain motor.
2. Remove chain track using the tool included in the weapon's field kit.
3. Inspect each tooth — replace sections with more than 10% tooth loss.
4. Lubricate chain drive sprockets with GX-3 weapon oil.
5. Reattach chain track and tension to specification (consult Armoury placard).

Thunder Hammer Maintenance:
1. Energy capacitors must be drained before opening the housing. Failure to drain results in electrocution sufficient to harm even Astartes-grade biology.
2. Inspect capacitor banks for swelling or carbonisation. Replace if affected.
3. The discharge node on the hammer head must be cleaned of carbonised residue with a ceramic brush after each battle.
4. Recharge cycles should be performed by a Techmarine only.
